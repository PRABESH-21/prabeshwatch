THE ROLEX STORY

Founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London, Rolex has grown to become the most recognizable luxury watch brand in the world. The company moved its base of operations to Geneva, Switzerland in 1919, where it remains to this day.

Pioneering Achievements

  • 1926 - Invented the first waterproof wristwatch "Oyster"
  • 1931 - Created the first self-winding mechanism with perpetual rotor
  • 1945 - Introduced the first wristwatch with an automatically changing date
  • 1953 - Launched the Submariner, waterproof to 100 meters
  • 1956 - Developed the Milgauss, resistant to magnetic fields
