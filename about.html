ABOUT GHADUMANDU

Your trusted Rolex authorized retailer in Kathmandu

OUR STORY

Founded in 2000, GHADUMANDU has established itself as Nepal's premier destination for luxury timepieces. As an authorized Rolex retailer, we bring Swiss watchmaking excellence to the heart of Kathmandu.

Our journey began with a simple passion for precision engineering and timeless design. Over two decades, we've cultivated relationships with watch enthusiasts and collectors throughout the region, becoming trusted advisors in the world of horology.
